Evaluation Rubric for Presentations

This rubric guides the evaluation of presentations within graduate medical education. It assesses various criteria, including:

  • History Taking - PC1, MK1
  • Hypothesis-Driven History - PC2, MK2
  • Patient Presentation - ICS1, PC3
  • Pertinent Physical Exam Findings - PC1, MK1
  • Assessment and Plan - PC4, MK3
  • Differential Diagnosis - PC5, MK2
  • Clinical Reasoning - PC5, MK3
  • Synthesis of Information - MK3, PC3
  • Oral Case Presentation - ICS2, PC4
  • Written Case Presentation - ICS3, PC4
  • Focused History Gathering - PC1, MK1
  • Concise Presentation of Findings - ICS1, PC2
  • Selection of Pertinent Positive and Negative Findings - MK2, PC3
  • Formulation of Accurate Differential - MK2, PC4
  • Application of Clinical Guidelines - SBP1, MK4
  • Effective Communication of Diagnosis and Plan - ICS1, PC5
  • Patient-Centered Communication - PROF1, ICS3
  • Clinical Decision Making - PC4, MK2
  • Effective Use of Evidence-Based Medicine - MK3, SBP2
  • Accuracy of Information Gathering - PC1, MK1
  • Prioritization of Patient Problems - PC2, MK3
  • Development of a Comprehensive History - PC1, MK1
  • Logical Flow of Presentation - ICS2, PC3
  • Ability to Adjust Hypothesis Based on Data - PC5, MK2
  • Selection of Appropriate Diagnostic Tests - PC4, MK3
  • Timeliness of Presentations - PROF2, ICS3
  • Incorporation of Patient Values in Decision Making - PROF1, PC5
  • Adaptation to Complex or Unclear Cases - PC4, MK3
  • Understanding of Disease Pathophysiology - MK1, PC3
  • Collaboration with Interprofessional Team - SBP1, ICS2
  • Justification of Differential Diagnosis - MK2, PC5
  • Reassessment of Diagnosis with New Data - PC5, MK3
  • Effective Handoff Communication - ICS3, SBP3
  • Integration of Psychosocial Factors into Plan - SBP1, PROF1
  • Application of Health Literacy Principles - ICS1, PROF1
  • Use of Clinical Prediction Rules - MK2, PC4
  • Adjustment of Management Plan Based on Feedback - PC4, PROF2
  • Continuous Re-evaluation of Patient's Condition - PC5, MK3
  • Consideration of Cost-Effective Care - SBP2, PC4

Comments and feedback from faculty should be recorded in this rubric to provide constructive guidance to students.
